# Code of Conduct

Be respectful, evidence-driven, and especially careful with financial and security claims.

- Critique code and ideas, not people.
- Do not pressure anyone to enable live trading or disclose balances, positions, credentials, or losses.
- Do not publish another person’s private information.
- Label uncertainty, simulation, demo evidence, and conflicts of interest.
- Report security vulnerabilities privately.
- No harassment, discrimination, threats, fraud, market manipulation, credential theft, or deceptive promotion.

Repository maintainers may remove content or participation that creates safety, privacy, legal, or community risk.
